Become a Better Goal Getter

Previous Next

Become a better goal getter to achieve your dreams.
Deciding: No matter what, you are going to achieve your goals. 'Come hook or crook, I am going to get this done.'
Planning what you decide: Getting your own plan in order of how you are going to achieve your goals
Beginning the Plan: Starting to take those steps
Progression: You see things coming along, you're learning.
Achieving: You hit your mark!

S.M.A.R.T.

Specif: You know exactly what you are shooting for
Measuring: You know when you have reached it. Find an objective way to measure your progress.
Attainable: You are in control of the outcome. If someone else's will is involved then it is not attainable . Also, you want to make a goal attainable by taking in account real limitations.
Relevant: The goal is important to you. Your goal should be something that you want to work on.
Time-Specific: You have a deadline. When you pin yourself down to a deadline, it holds you accountable.
