Fear vs Love

The epic battle! LOVE will always trump fear.
Be comfortable with your story. All stories have highs and lows. Make the choice of what 'voice' to listen to. Approve of yourself and allow yourself to make mistakes. How other people feel about you is none of your business, what matters is what how you feel about yourself. When you judge others, it showcases an insecurity of your own. You'll get what you give.
Be fearless and fierce within yourself. You are enough just the way you are.
Not all the people who dislike you are jealous of you. If you can't love yourself,you won't be able to love anyone else.

Giraffes are the magnificent creatures who can see everything from a bird's eyes view, they can look up and see the sky. However a turtle can only see the ground and be very pessimistic.
